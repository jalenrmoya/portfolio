About Me!

Here you can learn all about me!

My name is Jalen Moya, and I am currently enrolled in the Bachelor's program of Computer Science at Indiana University Bloomington, and I am set to graduate in May of 2025. I am also minoring in Game Design along with my major. I am originally from Jasper, Indiana, where I graduted from Jasper High School with an Academic Honors diploma.

Some of my earliest memories in life come from watching, and attempting to play, video games. My father was, and still is, a big gamer so everything I know I have learned from him. I grew up watching him beat the original Mario, play classic JRPGs such as Final Fantasy, and fall in love with newer titles such as Fortnite. When I was old enough to start playing games on my own, I became obsessed with sports games (NCAA Football, Madden, etc.), first-person shooters (Call of Duty), fighting games (Dragon Ball Z Budokai Tenkaichi 3), and sandbox games (Minecraft). From here, my love of games only grew. From that young age, I decided that I wanted to anything related to video games (or technology in general) for my professional career.

As I grew up and went through different phases, my love for games and tech only grew. I found myself in Computer Science courses in high school, and constantly playing new games trying to keep up with the industry. However, I did not let video games control my life, as I was very busy with Varsity Football, and having an after school job at the local grocery store, Houchen's Hometown IGA. From these two cornerstones of my high school experience, I learned the art of balance. I made sure I had time to do any sort of fun activities with friends/family/myself, but also made sure to do well in school and be impressive at work.
